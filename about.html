About After School

After School is a cafe mostly for students but everyone is welcome. Our mission is to serve students and adults alike with fresh, creamy, and strong (depends on their liking) coffee.In addition, starting next year, due to popular demmand, we will start adding more treats to our shelves. We hope to satisfy our customers' coffee cravings. You, and I sure "Can't wait 'till it's After School".

Real Inspiration

After School, the name, the description, basically everything is inspired by Melanie Martinez's 3rd album which is also named "After School Ep". All her songs were turned in to coffee/treats except the 7th song Bakery because it does not really fit to be a name of a treat/coffee; but some of Bakery's lyrics can be seen throught the webpages. The quote "Can't wait 'till it's After School" is actually a reference to Melanie's first virtual concert "CAN'T WAIT TILL I'M OUT OF K-12" and just modified some parts.

The reason I turned it into a cafe is because during Fridays, Pisay students (and teachers) go to a cafe nearby named "Cafe Dia" annnd students, and teachers go there "After School" (very punny). Me and my friends went there once and it was very obvious that it was our first time. Expensive coffee and drinks aside, it was very fun and we got to talk a lot in the cafe.
